My Favorite Philosophers

The greatest thinkers who have shaped human understanding

  • Socrates (470-399 BC)

    The father of Western philosophy, known for the Socratic method and the famous quote "I know that I know nothing."

  • Aristotle (384-322 BC)

    Student of Plato who made foundational contributions to logic, ethics, politics, and natural science.

  • Immanuel Kant (1724-1804)

    German philosopher who revolutionized philosophy with his critical philosophy and categorical imperative.

  • Friedrich Nietzsche (1844-1900)

    Influential German philosopher known for his critique of morality, religion, and contemporary culture.

  • Marcus Aurelius (121-180 AD)

    Roman emperor and Stoic philosopher, author of "Meditations," a work of personal philosophical reflections.
